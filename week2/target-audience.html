Target Audience

My target audience are the workers who need to know the weather condition for the day inorder to make vital decisions like what to wear, whether to take an umbrella, type of cloth to wear the children. My audence also includes outdoor event planners and virtually everyone who would need to fix dates and time for outdoor events. They would visit this site to know the weather condition of specific places inorder to fix the events on a date and time that would not be rainy.

Scenarios

  • Should I go out with my umbrella incase it will rain today?
  • Should my child wear a sweater or thick cloth today to prevent cold if it rains today?
  • Should I fix the event on that day? What if it rains and ruins the day?
  • What will the weather look like today?
  • I don't know if I should drive today beacuse I am not sure if it will rain and I can drive when it rains?

This site is the best place to find answers to the above questions and more.
