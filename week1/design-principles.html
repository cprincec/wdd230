We are going to look at three design principles below:

White Space

Google Homepage

google.com

The google home page applies the use of white space principle in the webpage to provide simplicity and focus on the main objective of the page which is to search for things. The page contains a lot of white space to allow focus on the search box.

Visual Hierarchy

FamilySearch

familysearch.org

Visual Hierarchy is the use of increased font size colors or font to lay emphasis on contents of a page in order of importance. This means that users' attention gets drawn to certain contents of a webpage before than other due to the font size, color or font used.

Hick's Law

Mp3juices Homepage

mp3juices.cc

The Hick's law states that the lesser the choices, the lesser the amount of time required to choose. The Mp3juices homepage makes good use of Hick's law by limiting the choices a user can make on their page to just the entry box for urls. Once a user enters the site it is almost evident that there is just one choice to make: enter the url of the video you want to convert and get it done.
