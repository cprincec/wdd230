We are going to look at 3 web design principles applied in various websites below:

White Space

Google Homepage

google.com

Google home page applies the white space principle to provide simplicity and focus on the main objective of the page which is to search for things. The page contains a lot of white space to allow focus on the search box.

Visual Hierarchy

FamilySearch

familysearch.org

Hick's Law

Mp3juices Homepage

mp3juices.cc

Mp3juices homepage applies Hick's law by limiting the choices a user can make on their page to just the entry box for urls. Once a user enters the site it is evident that there is one choice to make: enter the url of the video to be converted and get it done.
